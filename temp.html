The problem

Local problems stay broken for weeks. Sometimes months.

In most neighborhoods, reporting a broken streetlight means calling a number that rings out. Reporting a pothole means finding the right office. LocalVoicechanges that with a single scan.

🔇

No one to call

Residents don't know which department handles which issue.

📋

🔍

⏳

Weeks of delay

🤷

No accountability

No way to know if anyone is actually working on it.

How it works

Four steps. About sixty seconds.

  1. 1

    Scan the QR

    Point your camera at the QR sticker on any public asset.

  2. 2

    Pick a category

    We pre-fill location and category based on the sticker.

  3. 3

    Describe it

    Add a quick note and optional photo. No login required.

  4. 4

    Authority resolves it

    The right department gets it instantly — and you get updates.

Report an issue
